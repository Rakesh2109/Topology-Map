Network Segments & Actors

Threat Actors 💀

Malicious external hackers or compromised insider devices initiating attacks.

Zone A: Internet Edge

Public-facing. Contains Gateways and External Firewalls. Highly attacked.

Zone B: Enterprise IT

Corporate infra (AD servers, DNS, workstations). Often the initial breach point.

Zone C: Clinical Core

EHR, PACS, FHIR servers. The brains of the hospital data. High high-value targets.

Zone D: Imaging

CT Scanners, MRI. Heavy DICOM traffic. High bandwidth, legacy OS risks.

Zone E: IoMT Subnet

Patient monitors, infusion pumps, wearables. Crucial for life support. Low computing power.

Zone F: Vendor Area

Remote maintenance jump hosts. Vulnerable to supply chain attacks.
